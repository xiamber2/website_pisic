The problem

Pisic is built around one idea:

Safety should never be optional.

⦁ Safe Visualizer

⦁ Volume Peak Protection

⦁ No Unnecessary Animations

⦁ Made for You, Not for the Masses

Your music, your way. Stream without limits.
